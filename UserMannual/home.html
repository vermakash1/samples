Dashboard

A data dashboard is an information management tool that visually tracks, analyses, and displays key performance indicators (KPI) metrics and key data pointers to monitor the health of a business, department or a specific process.

The very first screen user will see after log in process is Dashboard.

The cards at the top in dashboard screen represent the key information about a business/channel partner. These cards are totally customizable and can be created as per the client requirement. In the above case, the card contains values for the following:

  • Total Super Stockists
  • Total Distributors
  • Total Retailers
  • Total Supply Points (DC)
  • Total DSDS
  • Total SKUS

Key Performance Indicators (KPI) - A Key Performance Indicator is a measurable value that demonstrates how effectively an organization is achieving key business objectives. Organizations use KPI’s at multiple levels to evaluate their success at reaching targets.

All Sales Orders - Sum of value of all the Sales Orders created by the user.

All Purchase orders - Sum of values of all the Purchase orders created by the user.

All Sales Invoices - Sum of values of all the sales invoices done by the user.

Pending orders for Invoicing - Sum of values of all the sales order against which an invoice has not been created.

Collected Amount - Sum of values of all the collections updated by the user.

Outstanding Amount - Sum of values of all the invoices against which collection has not been done/updated.

Important Note - The dashboard page is completely customizable. The KPI’s and the cards may vary as per as per the specific needs of a company. User can download this by clicking on download down arrow icon.


Inventory

Each Seller will have his own inventory as per the products mapped. Each product in an inventory will have multiple stages:

  • Current Stock – Stock which is ready for sales
  • Pending order Qty – This implies the qty which has come in the sales order for the SKUs, but it has not been billed/Dispatched from your end.
  • Damaged Stock – Stock which is damaged
  • Expiry Stock – Stock which is expired

“In Transit Qty (Out Bound)” and “In transit qty (In Bound)”

The Delivery is created by seller, but the GR is not done by buyer yet. Consider yourself as a buyer, the seller has created a delivery of 4 cases of "72", and GR is not yet done by you.

Before delivery

Current Stock = y (say)

In Transit Qty (Out Bound) = x (Say)

After delivery

Current Stock = y - 4

In Transit Qty (Out Bound) = x + 4

When the Delivery is done form the seller account, there will be reduction in "Current Stock" in seller account and Increment in "In transit Qty (Out bound)" for corresponding Products

Buyer Point of view

Till the GR is not done, the products will be visible in In Transit QTY (In bound) in the buyer account

Before GR

Current Stock = = y (say)

In Transit Qty (Out Bound) = x (Say)

After GR

Current Stock = = y + 4

In Transit Qty (Out Bound) = x - 4

The user can view his/her inventory from the below screen. The user can download the inventory report by clicking on download icon. Also, there is an option to download the Inventory tracking report (Opening and Closing stock report) by selecting date range.

Note: The Inventory will be automatically updated if any transaction is done.

Financial

Click on Collection in Financial section.Collection will show you the amount collected and pending by the distributor. You cannot make any changes to collections only able to see the collected and pending transactions and details (date, payment mode etc.)

  • Invoice Amout:This is the total amount that distributer(buyer) has to give to you corresponding to the order placed.

  • Collected Amount:The collected Amount is the part of the total Invoice amount that has been already collected. The collected amount is less than the invoice amount.

  • Remaining Amount:Remaining Amount is the amount that is pending or will have to be paid by buyer.

  • Invoice Amout: Financials > collections

Once user gets the customer return based on reason of return “Expiry”, user creates requisition to send it for audit the return. Below Requisition and Audit flow is only to see the status of Expiry Return.

Status of Audit for created Requisition of Expiry return can see in Audit section under Requisition menu bar. Audit is only for expiry return to validate the product expiry date and based on that status is getting updated.
